What is PhishGuard?

PhishGuard is a Safari extension that protects you from phishing websites, scams, and fraudulent content. It analyzes websites in real-time using multiple detection methods including AI, domain age checking, SSL certificate validation, and suspicious content detection.

How It Works

What does PhishGuard check?
PhishGuard analyzes multiple signals:
  • Domain Age: Very new domains (< 7 days) are often used for phishing
  • SSL Certificate: Checks if the certificate is valid and from a trusted authority
  • Content Analysis: AI detects phishing patterns in page content
  • Suspicious Keywords: Looks for scam-related terms like "guaranteed returns", "free money"
  • Brand Impersonation: Detects fake sites pretending to be banks or companies
  • Form Security: Warns if forms submit data over insecure connections

Common Questions

Why is a legitimate site flagged as suspicious?
PhishGuard may occasionally flag legitimate sites (false positives) for several reasons:
  • The site is very new (< 30 days old)
  • The site uses an uncommon SSL certificate provider
  • The site contains keywords that are commonly used in scams
  • The site is hosted on a free platform (Weebly, Netlify, etc.)
What to do: If you trust the site, click the extension icon and enable "Trust this site" to add it to your allowlist.
Why didn't PhishGuard detect a phishing site?
No phishing detector is 100% accurate. PhishGuard may miss some phishing sites if:
  • The site is very sophisticated and mimics legitimate sites well
  • The site is brand new and not yet in any databases
  • The site uses advanced evasion techniques
Always practice safe browsing: Verify URLs carefully, don't click suspicious links, and never share passwords or financial information unless you're certain the site is legitimate.
How do I add a site to my allowlist?
1. Click the PhishGuard extension icon in Safari
2. Toggle the "Trust this site" switch
3. The site will no longer be scanned

Note: Only add sites you completely trust to your allowlist.
Does PhishGuard collect my browsing data?
PhishGuard analyzes websites locally on your device. We do not:
  • Store your browsing history
  • Track which sites you visit
  • Share your data with third parties
The extension only makes external requests to:
  • WHOIS servers (to check domain age)
  • Tranco API (to check site popularity)
These requests are necessary for classification and contain only the domain name, not your full browsing activity.
Why is the extension slow sometimes?
Classification can take a few seconds because PhishGuard:
  • Queries WHOIS servers to check domain age (can take 5-10 seconds)
  • Validates SSL certificates
  • Analyzes page content with AI
  • Checks multiple databases
Performance tip: Results are cached for 10 minutes, so revisiting the same domain is instant.

Tips for Staying Safe Online

üîç Always verify URLs
Check that the URL matches the legitimate site. Phishers often use similar-looking domains like "paypa1.com" instead of "paypal.com".
üîí Look for HTTPS
Legitimate sites use HTTPS (padlock icon). Never enter passwords or payment info on HTTP sites.
üìß Be skeptical of urgent emails
Phishing emails often create urgency ("Your account will be closed!"). Legitimate companies don't pressure you like this.
üé£ Don't click suspicious links
Hover over links to see where they really go. If it looks suspicious, don't click it.
üí≥ Use unique passwords
Use a password manager and unique passwords for each site. If one site is compromised, others stay safe.

Troubleshooting

The extension isn't working
1. Check that the extension is enabled in Safari ‚Üí Preferences ‚Üí Extensions
2. Make sure you've granted the extension permission to access websites
3. Try reloading the page
4. Restart Safari
5. If issues persist, try reinstalling the extension
The popup shows "Unknown"
This can happen if:
  • The page hasn't finished loading yet - wait a moment and click "Rescan"
  • The site is on a non-HTTP/HTTPS protocol (file://, about:, etc.)
  • The extension doesn't have permission for this site
