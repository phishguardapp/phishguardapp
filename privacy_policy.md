# Privacy Policy for PhishGuard

**Last Updated: November 27, 2024**

## Overview

PhishGuard is a Safari Web Extension, aiming to protect you from phishing websites and online scams. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

## Data Collection

### What We DO NOT Collect

PhishGuard does **NOT** collect, store, or transmit:
- Your browsing history
- Personal information
- Login credentials
- Form data
- Payment information
- Cookies or tracking data
- Any personally identifiable information (PII)

### What We Analyze (Locally Only)

PhishGuard analyzes websites **on your device only** to detect potential threats:
- Domain names (to check age and reputation)
- SSL certificates (to verify authenticity)
- Page content (to detect phishing indicators)
- Form actions (to check for insecure submissions)

**All analysis happens locally on your device. No data is sent to our servers.**

## Third-Party Services

PhishGuard may query public services to verify website safety:

### WHOIS Lookups
- **Purpose**: Check domain registration age
- **Data Sent**: Domain name only (e.g., "example.com")
- **Provider**: Public WHOIS servers
- **Privacy**: Domain names are public information

### RDAP Queries
- **Purpose**: Fallback for domain age verification
- **Data Sent**: Domain name only
- **Provider**: Public RDAP servers
- **Privacy**: Domain names are public information

### Tranco Rankings (Optional)
- **Purpose**: Check website popularity
- **Data Sent**: Domain name only
- **Provider**: Tranco (academic research project)
- **Privacy**: No personal data transmitted

### AI Analysis (Optional, On-Device)
- **Purpose**: Detect phishing content
- **Processing**: Entirely on-device using Apple Intelligence
- **Data Sent**: Nothing - all processing is local
- **Availability**: Requires compatible Apple device

## Data Storage

### Local Cache
PhishGuard stores analysis results temporarily on your device:
- **Duration**: 10 minutes
- **Purpose**: Improve performance and reduce network requests
- **Location**: Safari extension storage (local only)
- **Content**: Domain verdicts and analysis results

### User Preferences
PhishGuard stores your settings locally:
- Trusted websites list
- Extension preferences
- **Location**: Safari extension storage (local only)

## Permissions

PhishGuard requires the following Safari permissions:

### Website Content Access
- **Purpose**: Analyze page content for phishing indicators
- **Scope**: Active tab only, when you visit a website
- **Usage**: Detect suspicious forms, keywords, and patterns

### Network Access
- **Purpose**: Query public WHOIS/RDAP services
- **Scope**: Only to verify domain information
- **Usage**: Check domain age and SSL certificates

## Data Security

- All data processing occurs on your device
- No cloud storage or remote servers
- No user accounts or authentication required
- No data sharing with third parties
- No advertising or tracking

## Children's Privacy

PhishGuard does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under 13. The extension does not collect personal information from any users.
